About Me

Rayyan Ahmed Khokhar

Introduction - Highschool

I was born on 2nd June 1994, in a city in Pakistan called Gujranwala, which is a part of the Punjab area in Pakistan. I barely remember my time spent in Pakistan as when I was just around the age of 6, my family moved to the UAE, in order to live a better life. Most of my childhood that I can remember is from the time I spent growing up in the UAE. I completed my primary school from Leens School and then my middle school till O level, that's 10th grade, from Sherwood Academy and then I completed the rest of my highschool and also graduated from Al Nahda National School. After which, I got admission in a Medical university in UAE. However, I decided to not continue studying medicine as I suspected that it was not something that I could do.

University - Present

After leaving my medical study I had some time before I could apply to another undergraduate field, during this time I took a 2-3 month course on 3D animation and design, which I really enjoyed and it also lead me to apply for an undergraduate program in Media Technology, thinking that I could further increase my knowledge of animation and design, however just before my main courses were to start I was informed that this program had been cancelled due to low interest from students, as such I had to choose another program to continue and end my undergrad. With my choices only being business or IT, I choose to go for IT network and security, which I continued studying and was expeected to complete graduate in 2019. Howver, due to various reasons and issues, we decided to move to Canada, as such I had to leave my then final year of undergrad and transfer to another univerisyt in Canada to continue and finish my studies. I was finally admitted to UOIT in late 2019 to a similar program as my previous one in UAE, however, due to university rules I was only given a maximum of 8-9 courses that transferred, as such I had to basically start my undergrad again in Canada, which I am currently in the process of. As of now I have another 1.5 years before I can graduate.
